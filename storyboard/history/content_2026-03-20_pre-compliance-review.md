## 1. Welcome

**Tagline:** The intelligent B2B networking platform

**Description:** Xpylon Connect helps professionals find, connect, and collaborate with the right business contacts. Powered by AI, built for privacy, designed for results.

**Key points:**
- AI-guided onboarding gets you started in minutes
- Smart matching finds relevant professionals automatically
- Enterprise-grade privacy protects your identity until you choose to connect

---

## 2. AI-Powered Onboarding

**Tagline:** Your personal networking assistant

**Description:** From your first login, the Xpylon assistant guides you through profile setup and helps you craft a compelling professional presence. No forms to fill — just a natural conversation.

**Key points:**
- Conversational profile building — tell the assistant about yourself
- AI suggests improvements to your bio and role description
- Your profile is automatically optimized for smart matching

**Icon:** sparkles-outline
**Accent icon:** chatbubble-ellipses-outline

---

## 3. Smart Matching

**Tagline:** Find the right people, automatically

**Description:** Xpylon uses AI-powered profile analysis to surface the most relevant professionals in your industry and adjacent sectors. No manual searching — the right connections come to you.

**Key points:**
- AI analyzes your role, industry, and interests to find relevant matches
- The assistant proactively suggests connections when you're ready
- Quality over quantity — every suggestion is scored for relevance

**Icon:** analytics-outline
**Accent icon:** people-outline

---

## 4. Privacy-First Networking

**Tagline:** Your identity, your rules

**Description:** When someone is suggested as a match, they see only your role, industry, and bio — never your name, company, or contact details. Your full profile is revealed only after you accept a connection request.

**Key points:**
- Anonymized profiles until mutual connection acceptance
- No cold outreach from strangers — you control who reaches you
- Full transparency: you always know what others can see

**Icon:** shield-checkmark-outline
**Accent icon:** eye-off-outline

---

## 5. Professional Chat

**Tagline:** WhatsApp-level messaging, built for business

**Description:** Full-featured messaging with everything professionals expect: reply threads, delivery and read receipts, typing indicators, and file sharing. Conversations are organized by topic for each contact.

**Key points:**
- Reply to specific messages with quoted previews
- Real-time delivery and read receipts (sent, delivered, read)
- Image and document attachments with automatic optimization

**Icon:** chatbubbles-outline
**Accent icon:** checkmark-done-outline

## 6. Voice and Video Calls

**Tagline:** Connect face to face, anywhere

**Description:** Make voice and video calls directly within the app. Professional call interface with in-call controls and call notifications.

**Key points:**
- One-tap voice and video calling from any conversation
- In-call controls: mute, speaker, camera toggle
- Push notifications for incoming and missed calls

**Icon:** videocam-outline
**Accent icon:** call-outline

## 7. Business Opportunities

**Tagline:** Create, discover, and manage deals

**Description:** Post business opportunities — partnerships, investments, supply contracts, and more. The AI assistant helps you write compelling listings, choose the right visibility settings, and manage interested parties.

**Key points:**
- AI-assisted opportunity creation through natural conversation
- Three visibility levels: open to everyone, network-matched, or invite-only
- Private or group chat modes for each opportunity

**Icon:** bulb-outline
**Accent icon:** briefcase-outline
